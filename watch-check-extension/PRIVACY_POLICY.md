# Privacy Policy — Should I Watch This?

**Last updated:** March 14, 2026

## Overview
"Should I Watch This?" is a browser extension that displays IMDB and Rotten Tomatoes ratings on streaming platform movie/show cards. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection
This extension does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What is stored locally
- Your OMDb API key (entered by you, stored in Chrome's local storage)
- Your rating threshold preferences
- Cached movie ratings (to reduce API calls)

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. Nothing is sent to any server we control.

## Third-party services
The extension makes requests to the [OMDb API](https://www.omdbapi.com/) solely to fetch movie ratings. No personal or identifying information is included in these requests.

## Permissions used
- **storage**: To save your preferences and cache ratings locally
- **host_permissions (omdbapi.com)**: To fetch movie rating data

## Changes to this policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here.
